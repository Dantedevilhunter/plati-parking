# Privacy Policy for Plati Parking

**Last Updated:** November 5, 2025

Thank you for using Plati Parking ("the App"). This privacy policy explains how your information is handled.

### Information We Collect

The App collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

1.  **Vehicle Registration Number(s):** The vehicle registration number(s) you enter and save in the app's settings.
2.  **Parking Configurations:** The combinations of city names, zone names, and SMS numbers that you create.

### How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively for the core functionality of the App, which is to:

*   Construct and send an SMS message to the correct parking authority number with your vehicle registration number as the content.

### Data Storage and Sharing

**All data you enter into the App is stored locally on your device only.**

*   Your data is **never** transmitted, uploaded, or shared with the developer or any third party.
*   Your data is **not** stored on any external servers. It only exists on your phone.
*   If you uninstall the App, all of its data is deleted from your device.

### Permissions Required

The App requires the `SEND_SMS` permission to perform its core function of sending the parking payment SMS on your behalf.
